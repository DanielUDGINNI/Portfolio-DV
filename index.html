About me

Hi! My name is Daniel Vazquez, and I am a passionate software developer and a recent graduate from Universidad de Guadalajara with a degree in Computer Science. Over the past few years, I have honed my skills in various programming languages and technologies, including Python, C++, Java, and web development frameworks. My journey in software development began with a curiosity about how things work behind the scenes. This curiosity led me to dive into the tech world early on, choosing to attend a high school that offered a technical program in computer science. At the age of 15, I enrolled in CONALEP, where I got my first glimpse into the vast tech universe.

I take pride in my ability to solve complex problems and my commitment to continuous learning. Whether it's diving into a new technology or refining my existing skills, I am always eager to expand my knowledge and take on new challenges. I like to think outside the box.

Feel free to explore my portfolio to see some of the projects I've worked on. I am excited about the opportunities ahead and look forward to contributing to innovative and impactful software solutions.
